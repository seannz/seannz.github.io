ABSTRACT
In recent times, compression of large language models (LLMs) has emerged as an
important problem for facilitating model deployment onto resource-constrained
devices, reducing computational costs, and mitigating the environmental footprint
of large-scale AI infrastructure. In this paper, we present the foundations of LLM
quantization from a convex optimization perspective and propose a quantization
